About Zeroflux

Your Trusted IT Infrastructure Partner

Zeroflux Technologies helps businesses build reliable, secure, and scalable IT infrastructure without the cost of maintaining a full in-house IT department.

We specialize in Linux server administration, network setup, system hardening, backup solutions, and ongoing IT support. Our goal is simple: keep your systems running smoothly so you can focus on growing your business.

Based in Ibadan and serving clients across Nigeria and Africa, we combine practical technical expertise with a security-first approach to deliver dependable IT services for modern organizations.
